Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git can be helpful for tracking progress in a large software 
project to ensure version control.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to meteorology to ingest historic weather data to make forecasts.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging will maintain the history of the merging branches but rebasing will overwrite the branches' history.
